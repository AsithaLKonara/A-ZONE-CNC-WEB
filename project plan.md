# 🚀 A ZONE CNC & Laser Solutions – Website Project Plan

## 1️⃣ Project Goal

Create a **futuristic, cinematic 3D-style website** that:

✨ Shows CNC & Laser work
✨ Attracts new customers
✨ Allows customers to request quotes
✨ Includes a chatbot assistant
✨ Looks modern and high-tech

Sections:

* Hero section with **3D futuristic background**
* Company name
  **A ZONE CNC & Laser Solutions**
* Tagline example

* Quick services overview
* Featured projects
* CTA buttons

  * View Works
  * Get Quote
* Animated stats

High Precision CNC & Laser Solutions
